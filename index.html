What is Tagia?

Tagia, as a succesor to previous endevors in server development, started in 2014 as a network of servers for Halo Trial, a free version of Halo Combat Evolved. It aimed at providing a near vanilla classic gameplay experience with quality of life changes to keep it fresh. Today we still host our Halo Trial recreation server, Tagia: Slayfest. We provide professional game server management, administration, and development as well as scriping/plugin development for little to no cost. Our network is growing every day adding more servers and projects in the realm of video games and beyond.

[↑] Halo: Custom Edition (More Info)

Halo Custom Edition, commonly abbreviated Halo CE, is a standalone, multiplayer-only, expansion for the PC version of Halo: Combat Evolved. Halo Custom Edition allows players to browse and play user-created content and maps created with modding kits. Modders would use the Halo Editing Kit to incorporate custom models, images, physics, and data into an easily distributed .MAP file, which is compressed and uploaded. The amount of custom content varies widely but is limited by the game engine's capabilities. (halopedia.org)
